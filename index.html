Two-Team Tiebreaker
TB 1
Winner of the head-to-head game between the two tied teams gains the higher position.
TB 2
The team with the most wins in the Preliminary Round gains the higher position.
TB 3
Best Goal Average using all Preliminary Round games: GF ÷ (GF + GA). Higher percentage advances.
TB 4
Fewest penalty minutes in all Preliminary Round games.
TB 5
The team that scored the first goal in the head-to-head game between the tied teams.
TB 6
Single coin toss determines the higher position.
Three-or-More Team Tiebreaker
TB 1
Most wins among tied teams — using only head-to-head games between the tied teams.
TB 2
Best Goal Average in head-to-head games among tied teams only: GF ÷ (GF + GA). Do not revert to 2-team tiebreaker.
TB 3
Fewest goals against in all Preliminary Round games played.
TB 4
Most goals for in all Preliminary Round games played.
TB 5
Fewest penalty minutes in all Preliminary Round games.
TB 6
Coin toss. In a 3-team coin toss, the odd team gains the highest position.
Key Notes
⚠
Points first: All tiebreakers only apply between teams tied on points.
⚠
Goal Average formula: GF ÷ (GF + GA) — not GF ÷ GA. Example: 10 GF, 4 GA → 10 ÷ 14 = .714
